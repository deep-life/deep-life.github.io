Dataset: GAN

The dataset of protein sequences used for GAN pre-training was obtained from open-source Protein Secondary Structure Dataset from Kaggle. The whole dataset contains 95,915 samples of varying length with up to 800 amino acids in a sequence. We have used sequences with up to 75 amino acids (= 225 letters in DNA coding). This resuls in over 24,000 training samples.

To limit the number of characters GAN has to learn, protein samples in this dataset were translated to DNA sequences. This translation is one-to-one (with a few expections that are not-siginificant for this task) and thus no information is lost during translation.
